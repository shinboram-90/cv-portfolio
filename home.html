About me

Passionate about everything I do.

They say: "Travel broadens the mind". Well, I guess this is true! From my numerous travels I would describe myself as an open-minded person who loves exploring new places and making new friends from all over the world.

Moreover, I'm driven by curiosity and challenges and because of that I can spend countless hours practicing my guitar or reading piano sheet music. I can spend countless hours in the kitchen cooking Korean recipes or making yummy cakes. I'm a very active person, I often go running and I'm always looking for fun outdoor activities like hiking, surfing or scuba diving.

The list goes on and on but my point is, since I've entered the world of programming I can't take my hands off my laptop! My desire for learning and growth keeps me going, as a matter of fact, I fell in love with web development.

2021

Le Wagon

With nearly 10 years in the hospitality industry, I decided to take a new career path and by chance I entered the world of programming. I recently completed the "Le Wagon" bootcamp in Bordeaux where I've learnt how to code with Ruby and the framework Ruby on Rails.

Moving to Australia was in my mind for quite some time so in early 2019 I finally embarked on a new journey. I've spent more than one year in this vibrant country working mostly at AccorHotels as a Guest Service Agent and a Human Resources Administrator. I was planning on staying for one more year but the pandemic got on the way, which by the way helped me to find my new passion. Yes, you guessed right, coding!

This fast-paced country never ceases to amaze me especially technology-wise.
